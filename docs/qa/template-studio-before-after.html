Email Template Studio: current experience and proposed direction

The current editor already supports visual and HTML composition, reusable variables, previews, test sends, and organization history. The proposed concepts keep that foundation while making publishing safer, versions easier to understand, and Resend an optional delivery sync target—not the source of truth for templates also used by Gmail and platform sends. Existing production templates are preserved in place and become the initial published versions; the Studio adds drafts and version history without changing their current identity or send behavior.
