## 2024-05-15 - Redundant Screen Reader Announcements in AI Chat Panel Action Buttons
**Learning:** In complex interactive components like the sliding `AIChatPanel`, there are many icon-only action buttons (e.g., stop generation, send message, approve/reject actions). While these buttons were correctly given `aria-label`s, the inner SVG icons lacked `aria-hidden="true"`. This causes screen readers to redundantly announce the generic icon name alongside the button's descriptive action label, cluttering the auditory experience.
**Action:** When implementing panels or overlays with multiple action states (like AI assistants or complex forms), explicitly verify that all icon-only buttons adhere to the accessibility standard of having both an `aria-label` on the parent `<Button>` AND `aria-hidden="true"` on the inner `<Icon>` element.
